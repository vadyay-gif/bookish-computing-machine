FAQ

A few common questions about how Orion AI works with individuals and teams.

Is Orion AI no-code friendly? +

Yes. We love working with no- and low-code stacks. Many of our first versions ship without any custom backend code, and we introduce engineering only where it creates clear additional value.

How do you handle privacy and data security? +

We follow privacy-first design: data-minimal by default, transparent about what is stored, and we use reputable AI providers. For businesses, we can support enterprise SSO and region-specific deployment options.

Where are you based? +

Orion AI is based in the UAE and works with customers globally. We’re excited about the region’s focus on AI innovation and use it as our launchpad.

Can you help with both internal tools and public SaaS? +

Yes. We build internal assistants and knowledge tools, as well as full customer-facing products with authentication, billing, and analytics.
